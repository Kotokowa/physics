梯度、散度、旋度与拉普拉斯算子公式及推导

梯度、散度、旋度与拉普拉斯算子在不同坐标系下的公式总结

——梯度、散度、旋度和拉普拉斯算子在三种常用正交坐标系：直角坐标系、柱坐标系和球坐标系下的表达式

1. 直角坐标系

  • 坐标: (x,y,z)(x, y, z)
  • 单位矢量: i^,j^,k^\hat{i}, \hat{j}, \hat{k}
  • 矢量场: A=Axi^+Ayj^+Azk^\mathbf{A} = A_x \hat{i} + A_y \hat{j} + A_z \hat{k}
  • 标量场: f=f(x,y,z)f = f(x, y, z)

梯度

∇f=∂f∂xi^+∂f∂yj^+∂f∂zk^\displaystyle \nabla f = \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} \hat{i} + \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} \hat{j} + \frac{\partial f}{\partial z} \hat{k}

散度

∇⋅A=∂Ax∂x+∂Ay∂y+∂Az∂z\displaystyle \nabla \cdot \mathbf{A} = \frac{\partial A_x}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial A_y}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z}

旋度

∇×A=∣i^j^k^∂∂x∂∂y∂∂zAxAyAz∣\displaystyle \nabla \times \mathbf{A} = \begin{vmatrix} \hat{i} & \hat{j} & \hat{k} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \\ A_x & A_y & A_z \end{vmatrix}

拉普拉斯算子

∇2f=∂2f∂x2+∂2f∂y2+∂2f∂z2\displaystyle \nabla^2 f = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2} + \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial z^2}

2. 柱坐标系

  • 坐标: (ρ,ϕ,z)(\rho, \phi, z)
  • 单位矢量: ρ^,ϕ^,z^\hat{\rho}, \hat{\phi}, \hat{z}
  • 矢量场: A=Aρρ^+Aϕϕ^+Azz^\mathbf{A} = A_\rho \hat{\rho} + A_\phi \hat{\phi} + A_z \hat{z}
  • 标量场: f=f(ρ,ϕ,z)f = f(\rho, \phi, z)

梯度

∇f=∂f∂ρρ^+1ρ∂f∂ϕϕ^+∂f∂zz^\displaystyle \nabla f = \frac{\partial f}{\partial \rho} \hat{\rho} + \frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial f}{\partial \phi} \hat{\phi} + \frac{\partial f}{\partial z} \hat{z}

散度

∇⋅A=1ρ∂∂ρ(ρAρ)+1ρ∂Aϕ∂ϕ+∂Az∂z\displaystyle \nabla \cdot \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial}{\partial \rho}(\rho A_\rho) + \frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial A_\phi}{\partial \phi} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z}

旋度

∇×A=1ρ∣ρ^ρϕ^z^∂∂ρ∂∂ϕ∂∂zAρρAϕAz∣\displaystyle \nabla \times \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{\rho} \begin{vmatrix} \hat{\rho} & \rho\hat{\phi} & \hat{z} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial \rho} & \frac{\partial}{\partial \phi} & \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \\ A_\rho & \rho A_\phi & A_z \end{vmatrix}

拉普拉斯算子

∇2f=1ρ∂∂ρ(ρ∂f∂ρ)+1ρ2∂2f∂ϕ2+∂2f∂z2\displaystyle \nabla^2 f = \frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial}{\partial \rho}\left(\rho \frac{\partial f}{\partial \rho}\right) + \frac{1}{\rho^2}\frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial \phi^2} + \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial z^2}

3. 球坐标系

  • 坐标: (r,θ,ϕ)(r, \theta, \phi)
  • 单位矢量: r^,θ^,ϕ^\hat{r}, \hat{\theta}, \hat{\phi}
  • 矢量场: A=Arr^+Aθθ^+Aϕϕ^\mathbf{A} = A_r \hat{r} + A_\theta \hat{\theta} + A_\phi \hat{\phi}
  • 标量场: f=f(r,θ,ϕ)f = f(r, \theta, \phi)

梯度

∇f=∂f∂rr^+1r∂f∂θθ^+1rsinθ∂f∂ϕϕ^\displaystyle \nabla f = \frac{\partial f}{\partial r} \hat{r} + \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial f}{\partial \theta} \hat{\theta} + \frac{1}{r\sin\theta}\frac{\partial f}{\partial \phi} \hat{\phi}

散度

∇⋅A=1r2∂∂r(r2Ar)+1rsinθ∂∂θ(Aθsinθ)+1rsinθ∂Aϕ∂ϕ\displaystyle \nabla \cdot \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial}{\partial r}(r^2 A_r) + \frac{1}{r\sin\theta}\frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}(A_\theta \sin\theta) + \frac{1}{r\sin\theta}\frac{\partial A_\phi}{\partial \phi}

旋度

∇×A=1r2sinθ∣r^rθ^rsinθϕ^∂∂r∂∂θ∂∂ϕArrAθrsinθAϕ∣\displaystyle \nabla \times \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{r^2\sin\theta} \begin{vmatrix} \hat{r} & r\hat{\theta} & r\sin\theta\hat{\phi} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial r} & \frac{\partial}{\partial \theta} & \frac{\partial}{\partial \phi} \\ A_r & rA_\theta & r\sin\theta A_\phi \end{vmatrix}

拉普拉斯算子

∇2f=1r2∂∂r(r2∂f∂r)+1r2sinθ∂∂θ(sinθ∂f∂θ)+1r2sin2θ∂2f∂ϕ2\displaystyle \nabla^2 f = \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial}{\partial r}\left(r^2 \frac{\partial f}{\partial r}\right) + \frac{1}{r^2\sin\theta}\frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}\left(\sin\theta \frac{\partial f}{\partial \theta}\right) + \frac{1}{r^2\sin^2\theta}\frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial \phi^2}

正交曲线坐标系下的微分算子推导

微元的写法

在直角坐标系中，微元写作 dx,dy,dzdx, dy, dz，形式直观；但在球坐标系等正交曲线坐标系中并非如此：r^\hat{r} 表示径向微元，θ\theta、ϕ\phi 不直接表示角向微元（量纲不对）.

  • θ\theta 方向微元应为 rdθθ^r d\theta \, \hat{\theta}
  • ϕ\phi 方向微元应为 rsinθdϕϕ^r \sin\theta \, d\phi \, \hat{\phi}
    记位移微元为：
    dl⃗=fduu^+gdvv^+hdww^d\vec{l} = f du \, \hat{u} + g dv \, \hat{v} + h dw \, \hat{w}
坐标系 方向向量 f g h
直角坐标系 x^ y^ z^\hat{x}\ \hat{y}\ \hat{z} 1 1 1
球坐标系 r^ θ^ ϕ^\hat{r}\ \hat{\theta}\ \hat{\phi} 1 rr rsinθr \sin\theta
柱坐标系 ρ^ ϕ^ z^\hat{\rho}\ \hat{\phi}\ \hat{z} 1 ρ\rho 1

梯度 \( \nabla t \)

全微分表达式为：

dt=∂t∂udu+∂t∂vdv+∂t∂wdw=∇t⋅dl⃗\displaystyle dt = \frac{\partial t}{\partial u} du + \frac{\partial t}{\partial v} dv + \frac{\partial t}{\partial w} dw = \nabla t \cdot d\vec{l}

代入 dl⃗=fduu^+gdvv^+hdww^d\vec{l} = f du \, \hat{u} + g dv \, \hat{v} + h dw \, \hat{w}，得梯度公式：

∇t=1f∂t∂uu^+1g∂t∂vv^+1h∂t∂ww^\displaystyle \nabla t = \frac{1}{f} \frac{\partial t}{\partial u} \hat{u} + \frac{1}{g} \frac{\partial t}{\partial v} \hat{v} + \frac{1}{h} \frac{\partial t}{\partial w} \hat{w}

散度 \( \nabla \cdot \vec{A} \)

散度用于计算 ∮A⃗⋅da⃗\oint \vec{A} \cdot d\vec{a}. 由于坐标系正交，面元 da⃗d\vec{a} 为立方体表面，体积元 dτ=dludlvdlw=(fgh)dudvdwd\tau = dl_u dl_v dl_w = (fgh) \, du dv dw.
前表面面元 da⃗front=−(gh)dvdwu^d\vec{a}_{\text{front}} = - (gh) \, dv dw \, \hat{u}，后表面面元 da⃗back=+(gh)dvdwu^d\vec{a}_{\text{back}} = + (gh) \, dv dw \, \hat{u}（含高阶小项）.
前后表面积分差为：

A⃗⋅da⃗back−A⃗⋅da⃗front=∂∂u(ghAu)dudvdw\displaystyle \vec{A} \cdot d\vec{a}_{\text{back}} - \vec{A} \cdot d\vec{a}_{\text{front}} = \frac{\partial}{\partial u} (gh A_u) \, du \, dv \, dw

同理，左右、上下表面积分差分别为：

∂∂v(fhAv)dudvdw\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial v} (fh A_v) \, du \, dv \, dw

和

∂∂w(fgAw)dudvdw\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial w} (fg A_w) \, du \, dv \, dw

总积分满足高斯定理：

∮A⃗⋅da⃗=∫(∇⋅A⃗)dτ\oint \vec{A} \cdot d\vec{a} = \int (\nabla \cdot \vec{A}) \, d\tau

因此散度为：

∇⋅A⃗=1fgh[∂∂u(ghAu)+∂∂v(fhAv)+∂∂w(fgAw)]\displaystyle \nabla \cdot \vec{A} = \frac{1}{fgh} \left[ \frac{\partial}{\partial u} (gh A_u) + \frac{\partial}{\partial v} (fh A_v) + \frac{\partial}{\partial w} (fg A_w) \right]

旋度 \( \nabla \times \vec{A} \)

旋度用于计算 ∮A⃗⋅dl⃗\oint \vec{A} \cdot d\vec{l}. 取面元 da⃗=(fg)dudvw^d\vec{a} = (fg) \, du dv \, \hat{w}，对应边长 dl⃗u=fduu^d\vec{l}_u = f du \, \hat{u}、dl⃗v=gdvv^d\vec{l}_v = g dv \, \hat{v}.
上部边积分 A⃗⋅dl⃗up=(fAu)du\vec{A} \cdot d\vec{l}_{\text{up}} = (f A_u) \, du，下部边积分 A⃗⋅dl⃗down=−[∂∂v(fAu)]dvdu\displaystyle \vec{A} \cdot d\vec{l}_{\text{down}} = - \left[ \frac{\partial}{\partial v} (f A_u) \right] dv \, du（含高阶小项），左右边类似处理.
环路积分满足斯托克斯定理：

∮A⃗⋅dl⃗=∫(∇×A⃗)⋅da⃗\oint \vec{A} \cdot d\vec{l} = \int (\nabla \times \vec{A}) \cdot d\vec{a}

通过分量分析，旋度分量为：

(∇×A⃗)w=1fg[∂∂u(gAv)−∂∂v(fAu)]\displaystyle (\nabla \times \vec{A})_w = \frac{1}{fg} \left[ \frac{\partial}{\partial u} (g A_v) - \frac{\partial}{\partial v} (f A_u) \right]

推广到其他分量，最终旋度公式需结合全部方向（此处略去完整展开，核心是通过环路积分与面元的关系推导分量）.

三重积

所有三重积的证明都一样，利用$\nabla (\phi\,\varphi) = \nabla (\phi\,\varphi_c) + \nabla(\phi_c\,\varphi)$，然后再结合不同的计算规则和行列式行列交换规则.

公式：

$$\nabla (\varphi \psi) = \nabla (\varphi \psi_c) + \nabla (\varphi_c \psi) = \psi \nabla \varphi + \varphi \nabla \psi$$ $$\nabla \cdot (\varphi \vec{f}) = \nabla \cdot (\varphi \vec{f}_c) + \nabla \cdot (\varphi_c \vec{f}) = \nabla \varphi \cdot \vec{f} + \varphi \nabla \cdot \vec{f}$$ $$\nabla \times (\varphi \vec{f}) = \nabla \times (\varphi \vec{f}_c) + \nabla \times (\varphi_c \vec{f}) = \nabla \varphi \times \vec{f} + \varphi \nabla \times \vec{f}$$ $$\nabla \cdot (\vec{f} \times \vec{g}) = \nabla \cdot (\vec{f} \times \vec{g}_c) + \nabla \cdot (\vec{f}_c \times \vec{g}) = (\nabla \times \vec{f}) \cdot \vec{g} - (\nabla \times \vec{g}) \cdot \vec{f}$$ $$\nabla \times (\vec{f} \times \vec{g}) = \nabla \times (\vec{f} \times \vec{g}_c) + \nabla \times (\vec{f}_c \times \vec{g}) = (\vec{g} \cdot \nabla)\vec{f} - (\nabla \cdot \vec{f})\vec{g} + (\nabla \cdot \vec{g})\vec{f} - (\vec{f} \cdot \nabla)\vec{g}$$ $$\nabla (\vec{f} \cdot \vec{g}) = \vec{f} \times (\nabla \times \vec{g}) + (\vec{f} \cdot \nabla)\vec{g} + \vec{g} \times (\nabla \times \vec{f}) + (\vec{g} \cdot \nabla)\vec{f}$$ $$\nabla \cdot \nabla \varphi \equiv \nabla^2 \varphi$$ $$\nabla \times (\nabla \times \vec{f}) = \nabla (\nabla \cdot \vec{f}) - \nabla^2 \vec{f}$$ $$\nabla \cdot (\vec{f}\vec{g}) = \nabla \cdot (\vec{f}\vec{g}_c) + \nabla \cdot (\vec{f}_c \vec{g}) = (\nabla \cdot \vec{f})\vec{g} + (\vec{f} \cdot \nabla)\vec{g}$$ $$\nabla \cdot \overset{\Rightarrow }{\mathop{\mathcal{J}}} = \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(\vec{e}_x \cdot \overset{\Rightarrow }{\mathop{\mathcal{J}}}) + \frac{\partial}{\partial y}(\vec{e}_y \cdot \overset{\Rightarrow }{\mathop{\mathcal{J}}}) + \frac{\partial}{\partial z}(\vec{e}_z \cdot \overset{\Rightarrow }{\mathop{\mathcal{J}}})$$

其中 $\overset{\Rightarrow }{\mathop{\mathcal{J}}}$ 是张量.

其他

$$\nabla \times (\nabla \varphi) \equiv 0$$ $$\nabla \cdot (\nabla \times \vec{A}) \equiv 0$$ $$\nabla f(u) = (\nabla u) \frac{df}{du}$$ $$\nabla \cdot \vec{A}(u) = (\nabla u) \cdot \frac{d\vec{A}}{du}$$ $$\nabla \times \vec{A}(u) = (\nabla u) \times \frac{d\vec{A}}{du}$$ $$\nabla^2 \frac{1}{r} = -4\pi \delta(r)$$ 高斯定律: $$\oint_S d\vec{S} \equiv \iiint_V dV \nabla$$ 即： $$\oint_S \vec{A} \cdot d\vec{S} = \iiint_V \nabla \cdot \vec{A} \, dV$$ 斯托克斯定理: $$\oint_L d\vec{l} \equiv \iint_S d\vec{S} \times \nabla$$ 即： $$\oint_L \vec{A} \cdot d\vec{l} = \iint_S (\nabla \times \vec{A}) \cdot d\vec{S}$$ 下面俩其实可以由 Gauss's law 推导得到： 格林第一恒等式: $$\oint_S d\vec{S} \cdot (\psi \nabla \varphi) = \iiint_V dV (\psi \nabla^2 \varphi + \nabla \psi \cdot \nabla \varphi)$$ 格林第二恒等式: $$\oint_S d\vec{S} \cdot (\psi \nabla \varphi - \varphi \nabla \psi) = \iiint_V dV (\psi \nabla^2 \varphi - \varphi \nabla^2 \psi)$$
